Our History

MTN Uganda Limited is a Ugandan telecommunications operator with the strategic intent of providing
telecommunications and digital solutions for Uganda’s progress. Inspired by the belief that everyone deserves the
benefits of a modern connected life, MTN has grown to a subscriber base of approximately 16.7 million customers.
